About 5280Farm

Welcome to 5280Farm - Your Trusted Source for Organically Grown Microgreens

At 5280Farm, we believe that healthy eating is a fundamental human right. Nestled in the heart of the Colorado Plains, we are dedicated to cultivating the finest microgreens, using methods that are both organic and sustainable. Our mission is to make nutritious, wholesome, and delicious greens accessible to everyone.

5280Farm was born from a vision to transform the way people think about and consume food. Founded by veterans who served our country with honor, we channel the same dedication and discipline into our farming practices as we did in our service. With a commitment to environmental stewardship and community wellness, we embarked on a journey to cultivate microgreens that not only taste good but also do good. Our farm operates on the principles of sustainable agriculture, ensuring that we not only nourish our community but also protect the planet.  

What We Grow Our farm specializes in a variety of microgreens, including but not limited to: Arugula Radish Pea Shoots Sunflower Wheatgrass These tiny greens are powerhouses of nutrition, packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. Grown organically, our microgreens are free from synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, ensuring that you receive all the benefits without any harmful chemicals.

How We Grow Sustainability is at the core of everything we do at 5280Farm. We use organic farming techniques that enhance biodiversity, conserve water, and build soil health. Our growing process is designed to minimize our carbon footprint and maximize ecological balance: Organic Practices: We strictly avoid any synthetic chemicals, relying on natural methods to manage pests and enrich the soil. Water Conservation: Our irrigation practices are optimized to reduce water usage without compromising the health of our plants. Renewable Energy: Our facilities are powered by renewable energy sources, reducing our dependence on fossil fuels and lowering our greenhouse gas emissions.

Our Promise We promise to provide you with the freshest, most flavorful microgreens, grown with respect for the earth and its inhabitants. Whether you are a home cook, a professional chef, or simply someone looking to add more greens to your diet, 5280Farm is here to bring you the best of nature’s bounty. Join Our Community Embrace a healthier lifestyle with 5280Farm. Connect with us on social media, visit our farm, or subscribe to our newsletter for updates, recipes, and more. Together, we can make a difference—one microgreen at a time.
